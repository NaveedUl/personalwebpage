About Me

My name is Naveed Ul Hassan. I am a graduate in Applied Chemistry with a Master's degree from Government College University, Pakistan, where I graduated with a 3.8/4.0 GPA and earned the first position (Gold Medalist). My thesis focused on the Ethnobotanical, Phytochemical, and Pharmacological Investigation of the Genus Salvadora.

In addition to my background in chemistry, I am a skilled Python programmer with expertise in material sciences, molecular modeling, simulations, and bioinformatics. I have worked on several computational and coding projects, applying my skills to areas such as drug discovery, disease prediction, and material characterization.
